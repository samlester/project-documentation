8.1 Closing thoughts

Content strategy is a new and exciting field in the digital industry, and one that is sure to grow as businesses become more reliant on technology to increase and maintain profits. As it matures and becomes more common clients will come to expect more and more from digital agencies in terms of data to back up their assumptions, more than can reasonably be captured using the mismatched set of tools which are currently used.

Currently, Scoop is almost unique in it's focus on bringing together tools for content strategy but I'm sure it won't stay that way for long. As content strategy is used more extensively, more manual and inefficient processes will be identified and superseded by specialised web applications. The feedback I've received from web professionals has been beyond what I expected.

There's a clear need for applications like Scoop; let's get to work on ensuring that this the first in a long line of innovations in the web strategy space.
